About us

Everything you need to know about us

"hachim" was founded in 2023 by two guys, named Julian Breinich and Philipp Kern, as a school project. The idea for this company was from Philipp, who, at the time, was (and still is) very interested in Chinese smartphones. The name of the company was an idea of Julian, because the school the project was founded in, is located in Hallein, a smaller city in Salzburg, Austria. The goal of this company is to make the smartphone market in Europe more diverse, and with that, more exciting. Because in these days, only few smartphone manufacturers dominate the European smartphone market, and because of that, there's also no real innovations in this area. That's a real shame, considering that there are so many smartphones worldwide, that are really innovative, but never end up in Europe.
